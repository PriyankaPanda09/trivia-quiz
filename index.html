About

Why use a trivia game?

Trivia is a great game to introduce at any party or event to bring people together and break the ice. A trivia game is adaptable and can be used in both personal and professional environments. It’s a fun activity for any event and will bring people together. In professional environments, it’s a great way to build team spirit between co-workers and test their knowledge. It also shows how team members work together and can be used to improve team collaboration and communication. In personal environments, it adds a fun layer to any party and brings people together.

How to play?..!!!

  • Players are either divided into teams or can play independently.
  • Once the teams are set, the Game Master chooses a question to ask the group. Teams choose an answer.
  • The Game Master reveals the answer.
  • Any team/player that has answered correctly gets the points designated for that question.
  • The team/player with the most points at the end is the winner.
